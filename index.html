About MindPulse

MindPulse is designed to solve a real and growing problem — poor mental wellbeing and burnout caused by unhealthy daily habits.

In Ethiopia, many students and young professionals face:

  • Irregular sleep schedules
  • High academic or work pressure
  • Long screen time
  • Lack of awareness about mental health patterns

Most existing apps only track data. They show numbers but do not explain what is happening or how to improve.

MindPulse is different.

It acts as a behavioral intelligence system that:

  • Detects unhealthy patterns over time
  • Explains the cause of stress and burnout
  • Provides personalized recovery suggestions
  • Adapts based on user feedback

"Instead of reacting after problems occur, MindPulse predicts risks early and helps users take action before burnout happens."

This makes it practical, accessible, and highly relevant for real-life use in Ethiopia and beyond.
